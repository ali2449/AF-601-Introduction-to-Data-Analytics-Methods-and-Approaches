Course Overview

Embark on a mystical journey through the Master of Science in Informatics and Analytics (MSIA) program at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro (UNCG). This 30-credit graduate degree weaves a tapestry of knowledge, crafting leaders and problem-solvers in the enchanted realm of data science. The curriculum, like a spellbook, comprises six core courses that forge a solid foundation in data science principles. Choose your path from six arcane concentrations: Advanced Data Analytics, Bioinformatics, Computational Analytics, Cultural Analytics, Geospatial Analytics, or Health Analytics.

Offered in both physical and ethereal (online) realms, this program arms students with powerful skills to manage, manipulate, and analyze structured and unstructured data, conjuring actionable insights. Key disciplines include programming for analytics, data visualization, statistical modeling, database design, and high-performance computing. Python Setup Guide

To wield the magic of Python, summon Anaconda, a mystical bundle that entwines Python with essential libraries, simplifying your initiation.

  1. Venture to www.anaconda.com and retrieve the installer attuned to your realm (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
  2. Cast the installer and follow the arcane instructions that appear.
  3. Unleash Anaconda Navigator and invoke JupyterLab to begin your coding quest.
  4. Create a new grimoire, such as IAF601_Notebook.ipynb.

Anaconda bestows key libraries like NumPy and Pandas for data manipulation. For guidance through troubled waters, consult the Anaconda scrolls (documentation).
